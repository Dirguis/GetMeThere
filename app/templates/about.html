GetMeThere

Hi, I am Damien, Insight fellow and GetMeThere is my project, designed to help people who wonder whether they should take the metro or a cab in NYC!

Before Insight, I was a postdoc at the Brookhaven National Lab on Long Island, trying to understand at a very detailed level how small molecules in the gas phase interact with light and more particularly high resolution lasers. This included understanding the frequency of light at which molecules absorb and emit (molecular spectroscopy), how they interact with themselves or other molecules through collisions (molecular dynamics) and characterizing the lineshape of the signal (lineshape modeling).

I have a PhD in Physics in the field of molecular spectroscopy from the University of New Brunswick in Canada, and a masters in Physics from the University Claude Bernard Lyon 1 in France.
